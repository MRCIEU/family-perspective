Family vs unrelated power / bias tradeoff in the presence of assortative mating

Cross trait assortative mating

\[ \begin{aligned} x_i &= \gamma_i g_{x,i}+C_i+u_i \\ y_i &= \beta_i x_i + s_y + C_i + v_i \end{aligned} \]

Covariance between a score for x and a score for y will depend on

\[ cov(g_{x}, g_y) = \frac{1}{4} cov(g_{x,o}, x_o) cov(s_{y,o},y_o) \rho \]

\[ \begin{aligned} R_{x,gy} &= \frac{cov(x,gy)}{sd(x)sd(gy)} \\ & = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\gamma \cdot \rho \cdot \beta_{gx} var(gx) \cdot \beta_{gy} var(gy)}{sd(x) sd(gy)} \\ & = \frac{1}{2} \frac{\gamma \cdot \rho \cdot \beta_{gx} var(gx) \cdot \beta_{gy} var(gy)}{sd(x) sd(gy)} \\ \end{aligned} \]
